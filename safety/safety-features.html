Advanced Safety Features

Relax... this home has it all, including the most high-tech safety features on the planet. Here's a walkthrough of all the safety technology of the Smarthouse.

Fire alarm

This is another sound feature of the Smarthouse.
A voice box will scream "Fire!" as soon as its smoke detector sences fire.

Anti-fire Snakes

The anti-fire snakes are special robot machines that put out fires whenever the Fire alarm goes off.

Safety hazard robots

The anti-fire snakes are similar to the Safety hazard robots, which are sent in case the Anti-fire Snakes can't handle a fire in Smarthouse.

Water Rats

These robots put out little fires anywhere in or outside of Smarthouse.

Indoor sprinklers

The indoor sprinklers put out very small fires.

Shudder feature

Another home security feature of Smarthouse is the shudder feature.

The Smarthouse automatically shudders when no one is inside
if the Smarthouse detecs suspicious movement happening right outside of it.
