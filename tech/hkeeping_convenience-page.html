Sound and Voice Features

Many appliences in this house are either voice-empowered or voice-activated. Here is a list of such amenitites:

  • Voice Clock - tells you the current time and reminders related to that time
  • Weather Box - placed close to the front door and reacts to the weather in different ways
  • Voice Calendar - tells you the current date and today's important event
  • Potry Reader - reads a poem of your choosing; located in the study
  • Music Player - play your favorite music
  • Sound recognition - a home security feature for pets; opens the door if it hears a familiar animal noise
  • Password - a classic home security feature; opens the door when you say the correct password
  • Fire Alarm - more info on the "Advanced_Safety_Features" page

    • The voice Calendar will tell you the current date at 7:30 every day.

Automated Cleaning and Robot Mice

Robot Mice:

The Smarthouse comes with many different appliences, which were built, programmed, and designed for your convenience.

The Robot Mice are special machines that are programmed for high-quality cleaning services.

Their job is to vacuum the floors, dust all surfaces, and wipe any dirt they can find.

Copper Scrap Rats

These machines are the heavy-duty versions of the robot mice.

They discard large peices of trash into the incinerator.

Incinerator

Located in the cellar, the incinerator instantly burns anything that is thrown into it.

Gardening

As with any property, you want your lawn to be green and mowed frequently.

Garden Sprinklers

The garden sprinklers water your lawn every other day.

Automatic lawn mower

The automatic lawn mower always mows your lawn once a week, but you can customise its schedule to suit your needs.

Kitchen appliences

No need to cook in this house! Don't even mention washing the dishes. The Smarthouse can easily take care of those things.

Automatic Stove

The automatic stove has the most features out of all the appliences.

It comes with a stove and can cook any meal of your choosing at any time of the day:

breakfast, lunch, or dinner,

and it can clean itself after each operation.

Automatic dishwasher

The automatic dishwasher can hold up to 150 plates, has 3 racks and washes your dishes in 70 minutes.

Aluminum wedge

The aluminum wedge will discard any trash from the kitchen table when you are finished eating.

It will also put the dirty dishes in the dishwasher, so that you don't have to.
