How Smarthouse was built

This house was made possible thanks to the work of over 200 people.

As a way to plan the house and desing it's exterior, the Smarthouse went through a 4-step art development process in it's pre-production stage.

5 concept artist worked together to develop what this house would look like:

When they finished, their work was then transferred to the 3D modelling team consisting of 67 people, who all contributed to the final desing of the Smarthouse.

Their completed desing was then sent to a trustworthy home building company.

Construction of the Smarthouse was completed in 107 days.

Finally, the interior desing team
(a total of 156 people, including our own electrical team and our delivery and furniture assembly teams)

worked on placing all of the high-tech furniture featured in the Smarthouse as well as connecting

wires and doing all the electrical work.
