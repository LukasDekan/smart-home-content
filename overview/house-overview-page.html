How Smarthouse was built

This Smarhouse was created, built and furnished by a team of over 200 people.

As a way to plan the house and desing it's exterior, the Smarthouse went through a 4-step art development process in it's pre-production stage.

5 concept artists worked together to develop what this house would look like:

When they had finished, their work was transferred to the 3D modelling team consisting of 67 people, who all contributed to the final desing of the Smarthouse.

Their completed desing was then sent to a trustworthy home building company.

Construction of the Smarthouse was completed in 107 days.

Finally, the interior desing team
(a total of 156 people, including our own electrical team and our delivery and furniture assembly teams)
worked on placing all of the high-tech furniture as well as connecting
wires and doing all the electrical work.

Floorplan

The Smarthouse has two floors. It contains an attic, a cellar and a sub-cellar. It also has multiple bedrooms, two bathrooms, a study, a nursery,

a garage, a kitchen, a patio, and a living room.

First floor

The first floor includes the kitchen, three bedrooms, two bathrooms, the patio, and the nursery. The garage is also part of the first floor.

Second floor

The second floor includes the study, and it also includes an entrance to the attic.

Attic


The attic is the most important part of the house.
This is where you can find the brain, which controlls
all of the technology in this house and stores valuable data.
The ceilings of every floor, including both cellars, contain memory tapes, which connect to the brain.

Cellar

The cellar contains the incinerator.

Sub-cellar

The sub-cellar is the only empty floor in the Smarthouse.
